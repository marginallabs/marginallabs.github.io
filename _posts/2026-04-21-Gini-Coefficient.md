# What the Gini Measures

The Gini coefficient ranges from 0 (perfect equality) to 1 (perfect inequality). It is derived from the Lorenz curve.

# Limitations

Two very different income distributions can produce the same Gini. Always look at the Lorenz curve, not just the number.
