# Why Regression?

Regression lets us measure the relationship between variables while holding other factors constant. It is the most commonly used tool in empirical economics.

# The Basic Setup

We observe outcomes $y$ and suspect they depend on some variable $x$. OLS gives us the best linear approximation of that relationship.
