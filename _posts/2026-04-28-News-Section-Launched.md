# Why a Separate Section

Blog posts and news updates serve different purposes. Blog posts are deep dives into economic thinking. News posts are shorter updates about the site and announcements.

# What This Means

The Blog page now shows only essay-style posts. All site updates and announcements live in the new News section, accessible from the top navigation bar.
