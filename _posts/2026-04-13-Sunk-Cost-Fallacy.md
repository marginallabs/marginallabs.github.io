# What Is a Sunk Cost?

A sunk cost is a cost that has already been incurred and cannot be recovered. Rational decision-makers ignore it.

# Why We Fall for It

Loss aversion and desire for consistency make us throw good resources after bad. The fix is to always ask: what is the marginal benefit going forward?
