Marginal Lab is now accepting guest contributions.

Whether you are a student, researcher, or professional — if you have an economics background and an idea worth sharing, we would love to hear from you.

Posts can be on any economics topic and should fit one of our three formats: [Essay]({{ '/blog/' | relative_url }}?format=essay), [Tutorial]({{ '/blog/' | relative_url }}?format=tutorial), or [Deep Dive]({{ '/blog/' | relative_url }}?format=deep-dive). No strict word count or formatting rules; we will work with you to get it publication-ready.

To get started, pitch your idea to [marginallab@proton.me](mailto:marginallab@proton.me) and we will take it from there. If you already have a draft, feel free to send it along.
